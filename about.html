About Me


Education

I graduated from Blanchester High School in May of 2023 after attending both Chatfield College and Southern State Community College for my Junior and Senior years. After high school I pursued a higher education in Computer Science at Northern Kentucky University (NKU). After a few weeks of classes I shifted my major to an adjacent one called Applied Software Engineering. I am currently a freshman at NKU.


Before College

I was born in Blanchester, Ohio. Blanchester was a little town without a lot of options for the students to excel, but I always strived to find the challenge I needed from school. I was in Band until 8th grade and had never missed Honor Roll until my Freshman year when Covid swept the nation. That instablity really got to me at times and made it very difficult to do well in school and I think I am still struggling to get out of that hole. Along with all the more personal issues I have been fighting through since I was a kid, I am proud of myself for making it this far at the very least.
